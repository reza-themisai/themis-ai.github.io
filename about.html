How it started

Our team has developed years of research focused on solving the issue of bias mitigation in AI. We have designed and developed solutions to identify and mitigate hidden biases of AI systems for facial recognition, autonomous driving, clinical trials outcome prediction, and financial time series modeling. The results of our research have been extraordinary and have motivated us to develop this technology on an industrial scale. Themis AI is a spinoff from DRL lab at MIT CSAIL and was founded in August of 2021.
